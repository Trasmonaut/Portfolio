TT Weather Predict

A predictive weather model for Trinidad and Tobago using historical weather data and XGBoost. Historical weather data was fed in on 16 reigions of Trinidad and Tobago into an XGBOOST model to predict what the future weather given a date and reigon might be in Trinidad and Tobago, based off what is was historically.

This proejct allows persons to have a refrence of what the weather in future might be, down to specific features/aspects for a given date. This would facilitate better planning of daily activies further in the future.

Tech Stack: Python, Flask, Jupyter, XGBoost, HTML
